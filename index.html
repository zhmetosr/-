ABOUT NCT

NCT (Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy band formed by SM Entertainment. The group is divided into multiple subunits, each with a unique concept and sound.

NCT U

NCT U is the flexible unit of NCT, with members changing based on the concept and music. They focus on experimental sounds and unique collaborations.

Learn more

NCT 127

Based in Seoul, NCT 127 combines powerful performances with urban music styles. The name "127" represents the longitude of Seoul.

Learn more

NCT DREAM

NCT DREAM represents youthfulness and energy with a fresh and dynamic concept. Their music is known for its catchy melodies and vibrant performances.

Learn more

WAYV

WAYV is NCT's Chinese subunit, focusing on the Chinese market. They blend K-Pop elements with Chinese cultural influences in their music.

Learn more

NCT DOJAEJUNG

A special subunit consisting of Taeyong, Doyoung, and Jaehyun. They showcase sophisticated vocals and charismatic performances.

Learn more

NCT WISH

NCT WISH is a special project unit formed for SM's annual Winter SMTOWN albums, featuring various NCT members.

Learn more
